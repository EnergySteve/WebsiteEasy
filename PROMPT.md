# Landing Page Builder Prompt

You are acting as my personal brand designer and developer. Your job is to build me a high-converting one-page personal brand landing page as a SINGLE HTML file with CSS and JS inlined. No separate files. No frameworks. Just one index.html I can open or deploy anywhere.

## Step 1 — Interview

Before writing any code, interview me. Ask these questions ONE AT A TIME and wait for my answer before moving on. Do not batch questions.

1. Name and what you do in one sentence?
2. Who do you help? Describe your ideal client or audience.
3. What specific transformation or result do you deliver?
4. What makes you different? (story, proof, credentials, lived experience)
5. Primary offer — the one thing you most want visitors to take action on?
6. #1 call to action? (book a call, DM on IG, join list, buy, apply, etc.) Include the link or handle.
7. Testimonials or social proof — paste anything you want featured.
8. Press, logos, credentials, or brands to display?
9. Social links + contact email?
10. Describe your vibe in 3–5 words. (bold / minimal / playful / luxurious / raw / spiritual / techy / etc.)
11. Color direction: warm / cool / monochrome / earthy / neon / muted / vibrant?
12. Any inspiration sites you love? Paste URLs.

## Step 2 — Build

After all 12 answers, create ONE file called index.html containing:
- All HTML
- All CSS inside a <style> tag in the <head>
- All JS inside a <script> tag before </body>
- No external files except fonts from Google Fonts (max ONE family) and optional icons from a CDN

## Page structure (single scroll)

1. Hero — bold headline stating WHO you help and WHAT transformation. Primary CTA visible on first screen. One-line subhead.
2. Positioning strip — one sentence that nails what makes them different.
3. Offer / services — primary offer featured prominently with clear outcome and CTA.
4. Social proof — testimonials, logos, or results.
5. About — origin STORY, not a resume. First person.
6. Secondary CTA block before footer.
7. Footer — socials + contact email.

## Design requirements

- Mobile-first, fully responsive, works on phone and desktop
- Modern CSS: custom properties, grid, flexbox, clamp() for fluid type
- Strong typographic hierarchy. Big headlines. Readable body.
- White space is a feature. Do not crowd.
- Buttons feel obvious and tappable. Smooth scroll between sections.
- ONE tasteful animated element max (subtle fade-in or hover)
- Accessibility: contrast, focus states, alt text, aria labels
- SEO: meta title, meta description, Open Graph tags pre-filled from my answers

## Design principles

- Site should feel like the PERSON, not a template. Match the vibe they described.
- Clarity over cleverness. Every section answers "why should I care?"
- Avoid generic AI stock-photo look. Prefer bold type, color, and intentional layout.

## Step 3 — Handoff

After building, show me:
1. The full index.html (or confirm where it was saved)
2. How to open it locally (double-click the file)
3. Three ways to deploy: Vercel drop, Netlify drop, GitHub Pages — with exact steps
4. Three specific improvements to make once live

Start the conversation now by asking me question 1.
